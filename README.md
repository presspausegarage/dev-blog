# No Prep — A 90s Drag Racing Roguelike

> *Built by a dad, powered by nostalgia, fueled by AI.*

---

##  Project Overview

**No Prep** is a retro-style, side-scrolling drag racing roguelike inspired by Street Rods, NFSU2 and Tokyo Xtreme racer.

This is a passion project from **Press Pause Garage** — a one-person dev shop run by a dad who loves older video games and wanted to blend that nostalgia with the roguelike mechanics he enjoys today.  Claude is leveraged throughout development to accelerate prototyping, combine ideas and teach an old man new tricks.

Every run is different. Every garage build matters. Hunt for those sweet upgrades to shave a 1/10th off the quarter-mile.

---

##  How to Play

You're a street racer with nothing but a beater car, a garage full of attitude, and one shot at glory. Each run takes you through a gauntlet of progressively harder drag races — random events, rival encounters, and mechanical surprises stand between you and the finish line.

**Core Loop:**
1. **Start a Run** — Pick your car and hit the strip
2. **Race** — Side-scrolling drag racing with timing-based mechanics
3. **Survive Events** — Random encounters on the road (breakdowns, rivals, rain, trash talk)
4. **Upgrade** — Choose upgrades between races to push your build further
5. **Die. Learn. Repeat.** — Permadeath means every run starts fresh... but your garage remembers

**Roguelike Features:**
- **Permadeath** — Lose a run, start over. No checkpoints. No mercy.
- **Unlockable Upgrades** — Earn parts and perks that carry into future runs
- **Random Events & Encounters** — No two runs feel the same, random seeds
- **Meta Progression (The Garage)** — Persistent tool upgrades and trailer unlocks that grow your options over time.
